First public release of Catty.

• 3D terminal scene with Maxwell the cat and a rat orbiting your prompt
• Grid-coord multi-pane layout — spawn terminals in any cardinal direction from any existing pane, no fixed limit
• Four per-pane surface modes: flat, cinemascope curve, Möbius strip, centre-bulge warp
• Citadel-backed SSH sessions alongside local shells
• Persistence: relaunch and your layout is exactly where you left it
• Soft purple selection glow that tracks the active pane through any camera angle
• Comical zoom range with a dedicated zoom slider in the right rail so scroll-to-zoom always works
• Cursor cat per pane that follows the trailing character

Open source under the MIT license at github.com/mochiexists/catty.
